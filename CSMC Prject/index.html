The Perfect Breakfast

The Perfect English Breakfast

By Max Cassett

Recipe Instructions

Begin your English breakfast by preparing the heartier components. Heat baked beans in a small pot over low heat, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, cook sausages in a pan over medium-low heat, turning them until they're browned and cooked through. In the same pan, fry back bacon until it reaches your desired level of crispiness.
Next, focus on the vegetables and bread. In a separate pan, sauté mushrooms until they're caramelized, then briefly sear halved tomatoes. For the bread, fry slices in oil or butter until they're golden on both sides, creating a delicious fried bread. If you prefer, you can toast the bread instead.
Now it's time for the eggs, a crucial element of any English breakfast. Fry or poach them to your liking – runny yolks are a popular choice.
Finally, bring it all together on the plate. Arrange the sausages, bacon, mushrooms, tomatoes, fried bread, eggs, and beans. If you've prepared black pudding or hash browns, add these to the plate as well. Serve immediately with a steaming cup of tea or coffee to complete your authentic English breakfast experience.
