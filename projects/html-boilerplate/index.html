The Perfect American Breakfast

By Max Cassett

Ingrediants

    Refried Beans
    Eggs
    A 3inch Thick Sirloin Steak
    Seasoning
      Salt, Pepper, Montreal Steak Seasoning, Butter, Garlic

Recipe Instructions

Cook the Steak: Season both sides of the steak with salt, pepper, and Montreal steak seasoning. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook to your desired doneness (about 3-5 minutes per side for medium-rare). Let it rest while you prepare the rest.
Cook the Eggs: In the same skillet, reduce heat to medium, add a bit of oil or butter, and crack in the eggs. Cook sunny-side up, over-easy, or scrambled, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
Heat the Refried Beans: In a small pan, warm the refried beans over low heat, stirring occasionally until heated through. Add a pinch of salt if needed.
Serve: Plate the steak, eggs, and beans together. Optionally, garnish with fresh herbs or hot sauce. Enjoy!
